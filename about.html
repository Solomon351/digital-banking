Our Story

We started with a simple question: Why is banking so hard?

In 2019, our founders — frustrated by hidden fees, failed transfers, and inaccessible financial tools — set out to build something different. SolCash was born from the belief that every Nigerian deserves a bank that actually works for them.

Today, we serve over 2 million users across all 36 states, processing billions of naira in transactions every month — with zero transfer fees and zero apologies.

We are licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria and backed by leading fintech investors who share our vision of financial inclusion for all.
